Welcome to Our Barangay!

Barangay Bagong Pook VI-C is a community dedicated to providing services and support to its residents.

Our Services

Our comprehensive range of services available in Barangay Bagong Pook VI-C includes essential documents and health support, such as the issuance of Barangay Certificates, the provision of Barangay Clearances,
the processing of Barangay Indigency Certificates, and access to a Free Clinic that offers medical consultations and essential medicines to meet the health needs of our community members.
